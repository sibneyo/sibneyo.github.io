Values:

Self Reliance Beauty Curiosity Growth Knowledge Compassion

I would describe the above values as my core values, they are the ones most important to me and they are part of what makes me who I am.

So how did these values come to be?

One thing people should know about me is that I am very headstrong, although my thinking can be flexible, I always follow the dictates of my own conscience.
Please take me seriously when I say my values do not come from my friends my family or society at large. They come from me and my firsthand experience of the world.
Yes when I was younger my values probably came from adults in my enviroment. Parents and Teachers. However early in my youth I decided that I was my own person, and that was that.

Why these values?

Alot of my value makeup is how I naturally am. The part the enviroment has to play is that it provided the structures for the 'me-ness' to flourish or dwindle depending on the circumstances and times of my life. I am very curious by nature, which leads me to seek knowledge. By nature, knowledge promotes growth. Beauty is something I am naturally drawn too, compassion I believe is the native state of all humans. My experiences in the world have taught me the neccissity of self reliance, so maybe this last value is not intrinsic to who I am.

Environmental Factors

Although my values are self deteremined. They have been encouraged (for lack of a better term) by people in my life. My Parents aided my curiosity as a grew up by allowing my to explore different hobbies, subjects at school, clubs, activities etc. They aided my self reliance by fostering my independence during adolenscence. They aided my compassion by teaching me to be good others. My friends aided my growth through good spirited rivalry at all sorts of tasks and activities. This includes my school mates who aided my knowledge when collaborating/competing in academic ventures. Beauty is very important value to me that contributes greatly to the richness and satifcation in my life. At many stages of life my understanding of beauty has taken twists and turns due the introdutction of novel insights about art, food, design, and other aspects of culture and nature.

An Ethical Decision

On one occassion I encountered a man on the street at night who had passed out and decided wait with him after calling an ambulance. Why? Compassion. Looking back I think it was a good decision because that man's condition could have worsened overnight.
